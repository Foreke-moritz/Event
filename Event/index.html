Why Choose CoreInventory?

Everything you need to run a profitable, organized, and secure small business.

Automated Inventory

Stock quantities reduce automatically after every sale. Get instant low-stock alerts and never lose track of your products again.

Real-Time Analytics

Track revenue, profit margins, and staff performance with dynamic reports you can filter by date, product, or payment method.

Cloud-Secured Data

Your data is protected by PostgreSQL Row Level Security. Each business sees only its own records — guaranteed isolation.

Printable Receipts

Generate professional thermal receipts instantly after every sale — with business branding, itemized lists, and payment details.

Staff Management

Create staff accounts with restricted permissions. Staff can sell products but cannot view costs, profits, or modify inventory.

CSV Export & Reports

Download daily, weekly, or monthly reports as CSV files. Filter by date range, payment method, or staff member.

Two Portals, One Platform

Strict role-based access ensures your business data stays safe and organized.

Admin Portal

Full Control

The centralized command center for business owners and managers.

  • Full product and category management
  • Track profit margins and revenue analytics
  • Generate date-filtered CSV reports
  • Create and manage staff accounts
  • Configure business settings and currency

Empowering Small Businesses Globally
