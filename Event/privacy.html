Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register and use CoreInventory, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and business name provided during registration.
  • Business Data: Products, categories, stock levels, sales records, and staff accounts you create within the platform.
  • Usage Data: Login timestamps, pages visited, and feature usage patterns to improve service quality.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the CoreInventory platform and its features
  • Authenticating your identity and managing your account
  • Generating reports and analytics within your business dashboard
  • Sending critical account notifications (password resets, security alerts)
  • Improving our services based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns

3. Data Sharing

CoreInventory does not sell, rent, or share your personal or business data with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for database hosting and authentication. They act as a data processor under strict security agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on secure, encrypted servers managed by Supabase (built on AWS infrastructure). We employ:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted database storage for data at rest
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) to isolate each business's data
  • Automated daily backups with point-in-time recovery

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your stored personal data at any time.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information via your account settings.
  • Deletion: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: Export your business data (products, sales, reports) in CSV format.
